Methodology

Eulerian Budget

NBMI ≡ E−P = ∇·IVT + d(TCWV)/dt

Positive NBMI = basin injects moisture to atmosphere.

Dry-Season Green-Up

NBMIanom > 0 while Panom < 0

Intact deep-rooted forests increase transpiration during dry/El Niño periods (Lee 2005, Myneni 2007, Bonal 2016).
